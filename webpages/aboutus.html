About Us

Weebookstore

Welcome to Weebookstore - Your manga bookstore at home. At Weebookstore, we take pride in being your go-to destination for the latest and greatest in Japanese and Korean comic books. Nestled in the heart of the Philippines, our bookstore is a haven for manga and manhwa enthusiasts, bringing the vibrant narratives and stunning artwork of these two rich storytelling traditions straight to your home.
What can you expect in our store?

1. Curated Collection:
Dive into a carefully curated collection that spans genres, from action-packed shonen adventures to heartwarming shoujo tales, and from epic fantasy worlds to slice-of-life narratives. Our shelves are filled with the most sought-after titles, ensuring there's something for every reader.

2. Authenticity Guaranteed:
We understand the value of an authentic reading experience. That's why we source our manga and manhwa directly from publishers, ensuring that you receive genuine and high-quality editions that capture the essence of the creators' vision.

3. Cultural Fusion:
Immerse yourself in the diverse cultures of Japan and South Korea through our extensive selection. From the dynamic storytelling style of manga to the beautifully illustrated narratives of manhwa, our bookstore is a celebration of the unique and captivating storytelling traditions of these two nations.

4. Community Hub:
Beyond being a bookstore, we strive to create a community hub for manga and manhwa enthusiasts. Join us for events, book clubs, and discussions that celebrate the art of storytelling and foster a sense of belonging among fellow fans.

5. Easy Accessibility:
Navigating through our online platform is a breeze, ensuring you can explore, discover, and order your favorite comics with just a few clicks. We're committed to making your shopping experience as convenient and enjoyable as possible.

Embark on a journey through the pages of our manga and manhwa collection, where every turn introduces you to new characters, thrilling adventures, and visually stunning worlds. Weebookstore is more than just a bookstore, it's a portal to the extraordinary realms crafted by talented storytellers from Japan and South Korea.
